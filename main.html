Our Believe

At PHCG we believe that, Jesus is God, the Son who came to save humanity through His sacrifice and resurrection. We hold firmly to the teachings of the Bible as the ultimate truth and guide for our lives. Our faith is built on the love, grace, and salvation found in Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

Our mission is to share the life-changing gospel of Jesus Christ with the world, transcending cultural, racial, and national boundaries. We are committed to being a welcoming and inclusive community where every individual, regardless of background or nationality, can come to know and experience the love of God.

Unity in Diversity

With members representing nearly eleven different nations, Pentecost Holiness Chirch of Grace celebrates the beauty of diversity as a reflection of God’s creation. We believe that the church should be a place where all people are embraced, valued, and united as one body in Christ. Discrimination and prejudice have no place here, as we live out the love and acceptance that Jesus demonstrated.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of light and hope, spreading the gospel to all corners of the world and fostering a community where people of all cultures worship together in harmony and love.

Sunday School at P.H.C.G

At Pentecost Holiness Church of Grace, our Sunday school program is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth of children by teaching them about Jesus and His teachings in a fun, engaging, and age-appropriate way. We believe that building a strong foundation in faith from an early age is vital for developing a lifelong relationship with God.

  • Learn About Jesus: Through Bible stories, activities, and discussions, children get to know who Jesus is, His love for them, and the lessons He shared.
  • Grow in Faith: Kids are encouraged to explore and ask questions, deepening their understanding of God’s word.
  • Build Community: Sunday school is also a place where children form friendships and learn the importance of kindness, compassion, and acceptance.
